My Hobbies

I like to spend a lot of time with my family. I have a wonderful wife, a 15 month old baby and the worlds greatest dog. They are an absolute joy and we all howl to each other when we get to start any extended amount of family time. We love to go to the beach or hike through the woods.

Whenever I can, head out to my wood shop where I like to get in the zone and build as beautiful of things as I can manage. It is a very peaceful past time. There is something I love about making something that will last longer than me.
